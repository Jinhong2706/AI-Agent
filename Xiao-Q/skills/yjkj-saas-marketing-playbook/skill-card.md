## Description: <br>
A curated marketing stack guide for SaaS startups with stage-specific tools, channel priorities, and metrics for growth planning. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Founders, marketers, and growth teams use this skill to choose SaaS marketing tools, prioritize channels by budget, plan content and paid acquisition, and track funnel metrics by company stage. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

Risk: Vendor and channel recommendations may be interpreted as security-reviewed or universally applicable business endorsements. <br>
Mitigation: Treat the playbook as business guidance, validate recommendations against the user's market and budget, and review external links before following them. <br>
