# 提示词模板和常用表达

## 通用模板

### 基础结构
```
[Subject], [Setting/Background], [Lighting], [Style], [Perspective], [Camera Settings], [Quality Modifiers]
```

### 详细结构
```
[Main Subject] in [ pose/action], [detailed environment], [lighting type] lighting, [time of day], [style reference], shot from [perspective], [camera lens], [color grading], [mood], ultra detailed, 8k, professional photography
```

## 主体描述

### 人物
- a beautiful young woman/man/elderly person
- a group of people, friends/couple/family
- a child playing/running/smiling
- portrait of [description]
- fashion model in [clothing]

### 场景
- a cozy coffee shop interior
- bustling city street at night
- serene mountain landscape
- abandoned industrial warehouse
- futuristic cityscape

### 物体
- vintage leather briefcase
- neon-lit smartphone
- blooming red rose
- intricately designed watch
