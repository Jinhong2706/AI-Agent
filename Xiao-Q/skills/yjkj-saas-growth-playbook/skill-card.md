## Description: <br>
The revenue-focused SaaS playbook for going from $0 to $50K MRR, covering pricing experiments, churn reduction, activation optimization, and expansion revenue tactics. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[gingiris](https://clawhub.ai/user/gingiris) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External SaaS founders, operators, and growth teams use this skill to plan MRR-stage growth work from first paying customers through $50K MRR. It provides playbook guidance for pricing experiments, retention analysis, onboarding optimization, expansion revenue, and stage-specific benchmarks. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Applying guidance involving surveys, session recordings, automated re-engagement, or customer health scoring can involve customer data and privacy obligations. <br>
Mitigation: Handle notice, consent, data minimization, sensitive-field masking, retention limits, and applicable privacy requirements outside the skill before using those tactics. <br>
